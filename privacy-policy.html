Fast QR Generator ("the Add-on") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Google Sheets add-on.

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The Add-on:

  • Processes all data locally within your browser and Google Sheets

  • Data Access: Only reads cells you select as source
  • Data Writing: Only writes QR images to columns you specify
  • No External Calls: No data is sent to any external service

The Add-on requires the following Google permissions:

  • View and manage spreadsheets: Required to read source data and write QR codes to your sheets

  • No data leaves your Google account

This Add-on does NOT use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics tools.

7. Children's Privacy

This Add-on is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
